The traditional Zettelkasten has 3 types of notes. The structure works like this:
1. Fleeting note
	1. Its a very raw note. The first thing that comes to your mind. It´s just writing what you think. In the case of [[Obsidian]], we´ll use the daily notes as fleeting notes.
2. Literature note
	1. When youre doing a more proper research. They´re usually pretty big. 
	2. They should be written in your own words. When we´re talking about #Obsidian that´s not something i´ll be using, because i dont have time for this, but its the ideal.
	3. For articles, books, vídeos.
3. Permanent note
	1. The most distilled part of your Zettelkasten. It summarizes a single idea in just a few sentences. It´s really concise, something so simple you could memorize.
	2. This is the distilled note.
	3. A distilled note doesnt have to be that short, it just has to be significantly more actionable and a significant summary of the stuff that really important to you.
Each note represent a different level of capture and distillation.
